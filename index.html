About the Show

Alex in Wonderland is the world’s first musically humorous stand up special powered by music and Tamil. Alex takes you into an hour of holistic humor and convinces that this world is indeed a wonderland.

This stand up special show is in Tamil and takes you on a journey filled with humor, music and nostalgia.We guarantee to fill your heart and soul in this one hour. So, don’t be shy and get your tickets booked now.
